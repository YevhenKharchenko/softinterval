Soft Interval:
Pulse Between

Act in the quiet between.
Everything just... pulses, you know? There's this rhythm to literally everything.
Light expands. Sound travels. Rings ripple outward in steady rhythm.
You cannot act during the beat.
You can only move in the soft interval — the brief pause between waves.
Miss the timing, and the world distorts.
Find the gap, and everything aligns.

Concept

So it's like a rhythm game, but instead of going nuts tapping everything, you actually have to chill and wait for the right moment.

Soft Interval: Pulse Between is not about tapping to the beat.
It’s about waiting for the silence between beats.

Each level unfolds in expanding pulse rings that define when you can interact. The whole thing feels like it's breathing - stuff just pulses in and out, you know? You observe, anticipate, and respond only during the safe interval window.

The tension doesn’t come from speed.
It comes from precision.

Core Mechanics

The system moves first.

Expanding pulse rings radiate across the screen
You may tap only during the safe interval between waves
Miss the timing and the environment distorts
Tempo increases and rhythms layer over time

At first, the pulses are wide and forgiving. As complexity builds, multiple waves overlap, creating narrow windows of opportunity.
You are not following rhythm.
You are navigating it.

Progression

From single pulse to layered rhythm.

Early levels introduce one clear wave pattern with generous timing gaps. You learn to sense the interval rather than react blindly.
Later stages introduce layered pulses with offset rhythms. Safe windows shrink. Overlapping waves demand anticipation instead of instinct.

In advanced levels, tempo accelerates and distortion consequences become sharper. The silence between pulses becomes thinner — and more valuable.

After a while, you'll find yourself knowing when those quiet spots are coming before they actually hit.

Modes

Multiple ways to enter the rhythm.

  • Level Mode

    A structured progression of handcrafted rhythm puzzles with increasing complexity.

  • Endless Flow

    Continuous pulse waves. Survive and maintain clarity as tempo gradually increases.

  • Layered Challenge

    Multi-rhythm scenarios where safe intervals overlap unpredictably.

Each mode teaches you something different about timing.
