Concept

So it's like a rhythm game, but instead of going nuts tapping everything, you actually have to chill and wait for the right moment.

Soft Interval: Pulse Between is not about tapping to the beat.
It’s about waiting for the silence between beats.

Each level unfolds in expanding pulse rings that define when you can interact. The whole thing feels like it's breathing - stuff just pulses in and out, you know? You observe, anticipate, and respond only during the safe interval window.

The tension doesn’t come from speed.
It comes from precision.
